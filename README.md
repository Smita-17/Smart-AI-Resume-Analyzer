In today’s hyper-competitive job market, landing your dream role requires more than just listing your experience—it requires **algorithmic precision**. 

**ResumeForge AI** is an open-source, Streamlit-powered intelligence engine that transforms static CVs into dynamic, ATS-shattering career assets. By merging advanced semantic analysis with role-specific LLM insights, it bridges the gap between how humans write resumes and how machines read them.

## ⚡ Why ResumeForge AI?

Most applicants fail automated screening long before a recruiter ever sees their work. **ResumeForge AI** turns the black box of hiring algorithms into a transparent, unfair advantage for job seekers by turning raw text into data-driven career narratives.
